Hot And Cold

In the game of Hot and Cold, the player gets a set number of turns to “find” a number. As the player guesses, they will be informed of how close they are to the actual number in terms of hot and cold.
  • Within 5: Very Hot
  • Within 6-8: Hot
  • Within 9-15: Very Warm
  • Within 16-20: Warm
  • Within 21-30: Cool
  • Within 31-40: Very Cool
  • Within 41-55: Cold
  • More than 55: Very Cold
